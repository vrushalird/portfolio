Hi there!


I'm Vrushali Daware, a passionate Senior RPA Developer based in Mumbai, India, currently working at Labcorp Laboratories India Private Limited. My journey in the realm of automation began with a keen interest in simplifying repetitive tasks and enhancing operational efficiency.


With of hands-on experience in RPA, I have worked closely with Process SMEs, aiding them in automating their daily routines to yield substantial ROI while minimizing resource allocation.


I have worked with different industry areas ranging from Healthcare, Finance, Audit, Retail, Manufacturing, Data Management and Data Migration sector, where I've successfully delivered solutions from simple task automations to complex end-to-end process automations. As a committed professional and a dedicated team player, I've taken on the role of mentoring junior RPA developers, implementing robust coding practices, and crafting innovative automation solutions using tools such as Blue Prism, Automation Anywhere, PowerShell scripts, VBA, and other equivalent frameworks.


Outside of the tech world, I indulge in various hobbies. You might catch me working on stimulating side projects that challenge my problem-solving skills, or perhaps engaging in some arts and crafts. I also have a penchant for travel, nurturing plants in my balcony garden, and unwinding to the rhythm of music.
